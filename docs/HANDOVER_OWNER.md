# Zota Owner Handover

## What Zota Includes

Zota is a connected service marketplace made of:

- `Zota`: consumer-facing app for discovering services and sending requests
- `Zota Business`: vendor-facing app for onboarding, publishing, and fulfilling jobs
- `Zota Office`: internal operations console for approvals, moderation, finance visibility, and staff management
- `beautifulmind.com.ng`: public website and legal/compliance surface

## What the System Does

The system supports the full marketplace loop:

1. businesses register
2. businesses complete verification
3. office approves verification
4. businesses publish services or assets
5. office publishes listings
6. consumers discover and request those listings
7. vendors fulfill jobs
8. wallet settlement happens on completion

## What the Business Owner Should Monitor

- number of active vendors
- KYC queue volume
- number of live listings
- request creation and completion rates
- wallet top-up behavior
- failed or abandoned operational flows
- office staff activity and permissions

## What the Owner Can Expect from Office

`Zota Office` is the internal control center. It allows:

- review of vendor verification
- catalog publishing and unpublishing
- monitoring of communications and notifications
- review of request movement
- office staff access management

## Governance Model

- office accounts are internal only
- super admin controls office staff accounts
- super admin can create, update, and remove office staff access
- access is role and permission based

## Release Status

The system is live as `v1.0.0`.

This means:

- the core marketplace loop is complete
- production deployment is active
- post-launch work should focus on stability, reporting, growth, and support

## Immediate Recommended Owner Actions

1. confirm who owns business operations
2. confirm who owns office administration
3. confirm who owns technical maintenance
4. set support escalation rules
5. set weekly KPI review cadence

## Key Risks to Manage After Launch

- payment provider failures
- vendor verification backlog
- delayed moderation
- staff access not being updated after role changes
- incomplete analytics for decision making
