# Zota Operations Handover

## Office Purpose

`Zota Office` is the daily operations surface for:

- KYC approvals
- listing moderation
- communication monitoring
- notifications and escalations
- finance visibility
- staff governance

## Daily Workflow

### Morning checks

1. sign in to `Zota Office`
2. check notifications
3. check verification queue
4. check catalog review queue
5. review active and expired requests
6. inspect finance anomalies

### During the day

- approve or reject KYC submissions
- publish or unpublish services/assets
- monitor request movement
- monitor communications and activity logs
- review staff activity where needed

### End of day

1. review unresolved queues
2. review unusual request or finance movement
3. confirm no incorrect office access remains active

## Office Permissions

Supported office areas:

- `OVERVIEW`
- `KYC`
- `CATALOG`
- `FINANCE`
- `TEAM`
- `MESSAGES`
- `NOTIFICATIONS`

## Super Admin Responsibilities

- create office users
- assign office titles
- assign access permissions
- remove staff who should no longer have access
- review office activity trails

## KYC Review Checklist

Confirm that the submission includes the required identity and address evidence:

- NIN or ID upload
- proof of address
- optional business registration certificate
- optional selfie if provided

Before approval:

1. verify documents are readable
2. verify business details match submitted identity where applicable
3. confirm the vendor is suitable for approval

## Catalog Review Checklist

Before publishing a service or asset:

1. confirm title is clean
2. confirm category is correct
3. confirm pricing is reasonable and complete
4. confirm uploaded media is valid
5. confirm listing quality meets operating standard

## Request Monitoring

Office should monitor:

- newly created requests
- accepted requests
- in-progress requests
- expired requests
- completed requests

If a request is stuck or abnormal:

1. inspect request state
2. inspect message history
3. inspect vendor state
4. inspect consumer state
5. escalate internally if finance or abuse is involved

## Staff Removal Procedure

When a staff member leaves:

1. super admin opens `Office Users`
2. locate the staff account
3. remove or disable access
4. confirm the user can no longer log in

## Support Escalation

Escalate to technical maintenance when:

- office pages fail to load
- finance data is inconsistent
- messages or notifications stop updating
- chat/call routing fails
- payment callbacks fail
